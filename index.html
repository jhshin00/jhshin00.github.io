About Me

I am a graduate student at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. H.Jin Kim. My research centers on developing generalist robotic agents that can assist people in their daily lives. Currently, I am focusing on integrating the knowledge from both foundation models and human demonstrations into robotic policies.
